About Us

Welcome to My Awesome Cart, your one-stop online shopping destination, inspired by the convenience and variety of global e-commerce giants like Amazon. We are dedicated to giving you the very best of products, with a focus on quality, customer service, and uniqueness.

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by Bhargab Das, My Awesome Cart has come a long way from its beginnings in Guwahati, Assam. When Bhargab first started out, his passion for creating a seamless online shopping experience drove him to do tons of research, so that My Awesome Cart can offer you the most convenient, reliable, and enjoyable shopping platform. We now serve customers all over India, and are thrilled that we’re able to turn our passion into our own website.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to become the best online shopping platform by offering an unparalleled selection of products, unbeatable shopping experience, prompt shipping, and exceptional customer service. We aim to create a space where customers can find everything they need, from electronics and fashion to home goods and more, all at competitive prices.



What We Offer

Quality Products

We offer a wide range of high-quality products across various categories. Each product is carefully curated to ensure it meets our stringent quality standards.

Customer Service

We believe that our customers deserve the best, and we are committed to providing excellent customer service. Our dedicated support team is available to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have.

Secure Shopping

Your security is our priority. We use advanced security measures to ensure that your personal information is safe with us. Shop with confidence knowing that your privacy is protected.

Fast and Reliable Delivery

We understand the importance of timely delivery. Our efficient logistics network ensures that your orders reach you as quickly as possible, no matter where you are.



Join Us on Our Journey

We are constantly working to improve and expand our services to better serve our customers. Your feedback is invaluable to us as we strive to make My Awesome Cart the best online shopping experience.

Thank you for choosing My Awesome Cart. We hope you enjoy shopping with us as much as we enjoy bringing our services to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don’t hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely,
Bhargab Das
Founder, My Awesome Cart
